Simping for IU

This is a website appreciating Lee Jieun, known professionally as IU. She is a South Korean songwriter and actress signed with Kakao M. Debuting at the age of 15, she has released a total of five studio albums and nine eps, ultimately becoming one of the most successful acts in the K-pop industry. IU is also commonly referred to as the "nation's little sister" due to her cute, bright, and innocent personality. More information can be found in the bio section
